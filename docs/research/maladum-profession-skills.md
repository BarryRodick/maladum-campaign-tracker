# Maladum Profession Skills

Extracted on March 30, 2026 from the local profession-board previews and two supplementary private archive board sheets:

- supplementary board sheet A
- supplementary board sheet B

Pip capacity is the number of numbered ranks printed for each skill. Starting profession traits are listed separately and are not counted as pip-tracked skills.

## Marksman

Starting trait:

- `Hawkeye` - Ranged attacks ignore cover. May fire at engaged characters without randomising target.

Skills:

- `Ambush` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use when enemy ends Move within 4 squares and you are in cover. Make a Ranged Attack against them. You may Dash before or after.
  - `2` Reaction: Use when enemy ends Move within 4 squares and you are in cover. Make a Move and then an Attack against them. You may Dash once before or after either action.
  - `3` Reaction: Use when enemy ends Move within 4 squares and you are in cover. Make a Move, an Attack, and a Move. You may Dash once before or after any of these actions.
- `Bullseye` - `3` pips
  - `1` Make short-range Ranged Attack. Do not roll. Scores `1` hit, ignoring cover.
  - `2` Make medium-range Ranged Attack. Do not roll. Scores `1` hit, ignoring cover and physical armour.
  - `3` Make medium-range Ranged Attack. Do not roll. Scores `2` hits, ignoring cover and physical armour.
- `Countershot` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use when targeted with ranged attack/spell, before resolving. Make Ranged Attack/cast spell against attacker.
  - `2` Reaction: Use at any time after target enemy takes first action each round. Make Ranged Attack/cast spell against target.
  - `3` Reaction: Use any time. Make Ranged Attack/cast spell against enemy with `+1` die, then Move.

## Guardian

Starting trait:

- `Guardian Bonus` - Increase starting Health by `1`.

Skills:

- `Brutal Assault` - `3` pips
  - `1` Make a Melee Attack with `+1` die. Lay target prone.
  - `2` Choose character engaged with you up to your size. Move them to any square in short range and LoS with no AoO. They suffer attack with `+1` die, adding `1` die if they hit a wall or terrain piece. Lay them prone. If they hit a character, it suffers the same attack. Passive: Ignore `Cumbersome` for melee weapons.
  - `3` Make up to three Melee Attacks with `+1` die. Lay target prone. Then Move.
- `Frenzy` - `2` pips
  - `1` Use before melee attack to roll `+2` dice.
  - `2` Use before melee attack to roll `+3` dice.
- `Impervious` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use when you would suffer a status counter. Discard it.
  - `2` Gain `+2 armour` until end of round.
  - `3` Restore `1` Health (even above starting level). Discard any status counters. You may ignore all damage, resist all spells, and discard all status counters suffered until end of round.
- `Onslaught` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use after scoring hits in melee. Make a Melee Attack.
  - `2` Reaction: Use after scoring hits with an attack. You may Move and then make a Melee Attack.
  - `3` Use during a Move. Ignore AoO. You may make a Melee Attack against each enemy engaged while moving.

## Magus

Starting trait:

- `Magical Knowledge` - Take the matching Maladaar Reference Card and mark spells as they are learned.

Skills:

- `Fortified Mind` - `3` pips
  - `1` Use before casting a spell to cast it while engaged.
  - `2` Reaction: Use when friend in short range suffers an attack. Use this character's magical armour. Pegs from this character and/or target may be used. Magic Die effects are applied to this character.
  - `3` Until end of round, all spells targeting friends in short range are resisted for free (unless they are `Unstoppable`). Attacks against friends in short range may use this character's magical armour. Pegs from this character and/or target may be used. Magic Die effects are applied to this character. Passive: When casting spells of level lower than rank, do not become Fatigued after `Mental Overload` result.
- `Frenzy` - `3` pips
  - `1` Use before melee attack to roll `+2` dice.
  - `2` Use before melee attack to roll `+3` dice.
  - `3` Use before melee attack to roll `+4` dice. Hits may be split between targets in range of weapon. Additional effects are applied to all enemies hit. Passive: Gain `First Strike`.
- `Intimidating` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use when enemy in LoS activates. Choose their target.
  - `2` Reaction: Use when enemy enters LoS or starts turn in LoS. Their turn ends and they are `Stunned`. Passive: As an effortless action, take an action with friend in short range. Then become Fatigued.
  - `3` All enemies up to this character's rank within short range and LoS are `Stunned` and `Terrified`. Passive: Gain `Terrifying`.

## Rogue

Skills:

- `Camouflage` - `3` pips
  - `1` While in contact with a wall you cannot be targeted until end of round. Enemies can move through adjacent squares without stopping or suffering AoO but cannot end a Move in contact with you. You do not block LoS.
  - `2` Make a Move or Ranged Attack. Then, while in contact with any terrain you cannot be targeted until end of round, as above.
  - `3` While not in contact with an enemy you cannot be targeted until end of round, as above. Passive Reaction: At any time while this skill is active (including midway through an enemy Move) make a Ranged Attack and become Fatigued.
- `Distraction` - `3` pips
  - `1` A character in medium range and LoS is Fatigued.
  - `2` Make a Ranged Attack/Throw. Target or other enemy in short range of target suffers `2` Fatigue in addition to other effects. Then Move.
  - `3` Choose character in medium range and LoS. Move them (may not engage or cause harm). They suffer `2` Fatigue. In any order, before or after moving target, you may Move and Attack.
- `Duck for Cover` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use when targeted with ranged attack or spell when within `4` squares of cover. Move into cover.
  - `2` Reaction: Use when targeted with ranged attack or spell when within `4` squares of cover. Move into cover and ignore effects of attack.
  - `3` Reaction: Use any time. In any order, Move into cover ignoring AoO and Attack. You cannot be targeted until end of round while still.
- `Light Fingers` - `3` pips
  - `1` Reaction: Use after suffering zero-damage melee attack. Take item from attacker (not attacking weapon), dropping an item if no space.
  - `2` Move into contact and `Pickpocket`. Then Move with no AoO.
  - `3` Passive: You may `Pickpocket` as standard.

`Pickpocket`:

- When in contact with character, roll combat die.
- Skull: No effect.
- Blank: Scatter random item.
- Single success icon: Take non-large item.
- Double success icon: Take item of choice. Target is prone.
- Target may spend Skill peg to cancel effect.
